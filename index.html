About
The work, in plain terms

I am Managing Partner of the Centre for Digital Development and Innovation Research (CDDIR) within AREAi, and Founder of Digital Shield Social Impact Initiative (DigiShield). Over the past seven years, I have designed and led programmes reaching over 30,000 people across Nigeria, the DRC, and Rwanda; from foundational literacy for children in conflict zones to AI-powered teacher development and climate sustainability research.

My work is grounded in one core belief: the most important question in development is not what sounds good, but what works, for whom, and how we know. That question drives my research, my programmes, and my writing.

I serve as Co-Chair of the AI, Digital Learning and Digital Transformation Workstream at the UNESCO SDG4 Youth and Student Network, Programme Officer at the COP30 Children and Youth Pavilion, and member of Chatham House Common Futures Conversations. I am completing executive education in AI and Digital Transformation in Government at Oxford's Saïd Business School in partnership with UNESCO.
