About

I am Managing Partner of the Centre for Digital Development and Innovation Research (CDDIR) within AREAi, and Founder of Digital Shield Social Impact Initiative (DigiShield). Over the past seven years, I have designed and led programmes reaching over 30,000 people across Nigeria, the DRC, and internationally — from foundational literacy for children in conflict zones to AI-powered teacher development and climate sustainability research.

My work is grounded in one core belief: the most important question in development is not what sounds good, but what works, for whom, and how we know. That question drives my research, my programmes, and my writing.

I serve as Co-Chair of the AI, Digital Learning and Digital Transformation Workstream at the UNESCO SDG4 Youth and Student Network, Programme Officer at the COP30 Children and Youth Pavilion, and member of Chatham House Common Futures Conversations. I am completing executive education in AI and Digital Transformation in Government at Oxford's Saïd Business School in partnership with UNESCO.

Programmes & Products
FastTrack
Accelerated foundational literacy and numeracy for 25,000+ children in IDP and refugee camps across Nigeria and the DRC. Uses TaRL methodology with text-to-speech and bilingual instruction.
★ UNESCO Confucius Prize 2024 · WISE Finalist 2025
Afara.AI
WhatsApp and Telegram-based AI micro-CPD tool for teachers, grounded in TaRL methodology. Built for low-connectivity environments. Deployed to 625+ teachers across Nigeria and DRC.
CarbonTrac.AI
Africa's first digital carbon footprint calculator for the technology sector. Enables organisations to measure, visualise, and report technology-led carbon emissions and identify mitigation pathways.
★ QS ImpACT Award Climate Education 2025
DigiShield DSA
Digital Skills Accelerator reaching 5,000+ women and girls, including the WebWise Women track for IDP and refugee settings. Civic education on digital safety, rights, and online harm reduction.
★ Equals in Tech Award 2022
FoundaMENTA
Teacher continuous professional development programme integrating structured pedagogy, adaptive learning, and community-led peer mentoring networks for educators in underserved contexts.
SCALE for Net Zero
UKFCDO-funded programme on technology-led GHG emissions in Nigeria. Includes LMS development, CarbonTrac.AI, primary research, and stakeholder policy engagement at national and international levels.
Book in Progress
Visible to Everyone But Yourself
Privacy, Power, and Who's Really Writing Your Story
A work of cultural criticism and narrative nonfiction examining how digital platforms and AI systems shape human identity, behaviour, and power — invisibly, and by design. Written from Nigeria, for a global audience underserved by existing literature on surveillance capitalism. The book argues that privacy is not about secrecy — it is about ownership of one's own story. Eight draft chapters complete.
